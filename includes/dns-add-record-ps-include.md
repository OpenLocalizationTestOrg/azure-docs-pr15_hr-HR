### <a name="create-an-aaaa-record-set-with-a-single-record"></a>Stvaranje zapisa AAAA postavljen s jednim zapisom

    $rs = New-AzureRmDnsRecordSet -Name "test-aaaa" -RecordType AAAA -Ttl 60 -ZoneName contoso.com -ResourceGroupName MyAzureResourceGroup
    Add-AzureRmDnsRecordConfig -RecordSet $rs -Ipv6Address "2607:f8b0:4009:1803::1005"
    Set-AzureRmDnsRecordSet -RecordSet $rs

### <a name="create-an-mx-record-set-with-a-single-record"></a>Stvaranje MX zapisa postavite s jednim zapisom

U ovom primjeru se koristi naziv skup zapisa "@" za stvaranje MX zapisa na vrh zone (u tom slučaju "contoso.com"). To je uobičajena za MX zapise.

    $rs = New-AzureRmDnsRecordSet -Name "@" -RecordType MX -Ttl 60 -ZoneName contoso.com -ResourceGroupName MyAzureResourceGroup
    Add-AzureRmDnsRecordConfig -RecordSet $rs -Exchange "mail.contoso.com" -Preference 5
    Set-AzureRmDnsRecordSet -RecordSet $rs

### <a name="create-a-ptr-record-set-with-a-single-record"></a>Stvaranje PTR zapis postavljen s jednim zapisom
U ovom slučaju "Moje-arpa-zone.com" predstavlja zone ARPA koji predstavlja vaš raspon IP.  Svaki PTR zapis u ovoj zoni odgovara IP adresa u dosegu IP.  

    $rs = New-AzureRmDnsRecordSet -Name "10" -RecordType PTR -Ttl 3600 -ZoneName my-arpa-zone.com -ResourceGroupName MyAzureResourceGroup
    Add-AzureRmDnsRecordConfig -RecordSet $rs -Ptrdname "myservice.contoso.com"
    Set-AzureRmDnsRecordSet -RecordSet $rs

### <a name="create-an-srv-record-set-with-a-single-record"></a>Stvori SRV zapis postavljen s jednim zapisom

Ako stvarate SRV zapis u korijenskom direktoriju u zonu, jednostavno odrediti *_service* i *_protocol* u naziv zapisa. Nema potrebe da biste uključili "@" u naziv zapisa.

    $rs = New-AzureRmDnsRecordSet -Name "_sip._tls" -RecordType SRV -Ttl 60 -ZoneName contoso.com -ResourceGroupName MyAzureResourceGroup
    Add-AzureRmDnsRecordConfig -RecordSet $rs –Priority 0 –Weight 5 –Port 8080 –Target "sip.contoso.com"
    Set-AzureRmDnsRecordSet -RecordSet $rs
